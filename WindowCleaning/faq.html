Frequently Asked Questions

Answers to common questions about our services

For most homes and businesses, we recommend professional window cleaning at least twice a year. High-traffic or coastal areas may benefit from more frequent service.
For exterior-only services, you do not need to be home as long as we have access to the windows. For interior cleaning, someone will need to be present.
We kindly ask for at least 24 hours' notice for cancellations or rescheduling. No-shows may be subject to a fee.
